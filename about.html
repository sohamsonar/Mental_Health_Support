About Us

The Mental Health Foundation is a non-profit that partners with organizations to help communities thrive by providing education and healthcare. Mental Health Foundation launched a mental health helpline in India in 2009 to offer free psychological counselling and crisis mediation to anyone who is experiencing distress due to depression, trauma, mood disorders, chronic illness, and relationship conflict, to name a few. 14 years of supporting those who need mental and emotional counselling, we have facilitated 1 million+ conversations to date. Our emergency mental health support service is accessible 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Our Founders are working to create affordable infrastructure in California for those struggling with addiction through the creation of The Bricks Foundation.

This Is What We Do

Provide quality mental health services and support by carefully vetting and selecting a team of experts.

Respond and treat every individual with respect and dignity, while empowering them to have their own voice.

Continually learning, researching and creating more efficient methods to reach and serve the community optimally.

Benefits of Mental Health Foundation Paid Consultation

  • An affordable service staffed by highly qualified and trained mental health experts.
  • Therapy sessions from a comfortable space where you can freely share your thoughts.
  • Accessible even to those lacking transportation services or residing in rural areas.
  • Ability to choose from a multilingual pool of counsellors.
  • Keeps counselling discreet by allowing you to meet your counsellor from home.
